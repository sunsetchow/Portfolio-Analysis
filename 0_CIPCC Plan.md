## 10-Day Preparation Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-2)
**Goal:** Understand the case, align on approach, divide work

#### Day 1: Case Analysis & Kickoff
- [ ] **All:** Read case materials thoroughly (2-3 hours each)
- [ ] **All:** Team kickoff meeting (1.5 hours)
  - Discuss initial impressions
  - Identify key constraints (client profile, risk tolerance, time horizon)
  - Agree on investment philosophy approach
  - Confirm role assignments
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Create project tracker and timeline
- [ ] **Richard:** Set up shared folder structure

#### Day 2: Research Foundation
- [ ] **Taichi:** Review current market conditions, yield curves, asset class expectations
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Research comparable portfolio strategies (endowment model, liability-driven, etc.)
- [ ] **Issei:** Study past CIPCC winning approaches (if available)
- [ ] **Richard:** Gather economic outlook data, create market environment summary
- [ ] **All:** Evening sync call (30 min) — share findings

### Phase 2: Strategy Development (Days 3-5)
**Goal:** Build investment thesis and portfolio framework

#### Day 3: Investment Thesis
- [ ] **All:** Strategy workshop (2 hours)
  - Define client objectives and constraints
  - Establish Investment Policy Statement (IPS) framework
  - Agree on strategic asset allocation ranges
- [ ] **Taichi:** Begin quantitative modeling (mean-variance optimization)
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Draft investment thesis structure

#### Day 4: Portfolio Construction
- [ ] **Taichi:** Complete initial asset allocation model with risk metrics
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Develop asset class selection rationale
- [ ] **Issei:** Research specific fund/ETF recommendations
- [ ] **Richard:** Analyze historical performance data for proposed allocations
- [ ] **All:** Working session (2 hours) — review Taichi's model, iterate

#### Day 5: Risk Analysis & Stress Testing
- [ ] **Taichi:** Run stress tests (2008 crisis, COVID crash, rate hike scenarios)
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Document risk management framework
- [ ] **Issei:** Prepare rebalancing and monitoring recommendations
- [ ] **Richard:** Create performance attribution analysis
- [ ] **All:** Midpoint review meeting (1.5 hours) — validate approach

### Phase 3: Presentation Development (Days 6-8)
**Goal:** Build compelling presentation and written summary

#### Day 6: Presentation Structure
- [ ] **Issei:** Create presentation outline and narrative arc
- [ ] **All:** Content workshop (2 hours) — fill in slide content together
- [ ] **Richard:** Begin 2-page written summary draft

#### Day 7: Slide Production
- [ ] **Richard:** Design slides with visualizations
- [ ] **Taichi:** Create quantitative exhibits (efficient frontier, risk decomposition)
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Refine investment thesis slides
- [ ] **Issei:** Write speaker notes and transitions
- [ ] **All:** First full deck review (1 hour)

#### Day 8: Written Summary & Deck Polish
- [ ] **Richard:** Finalize 2-page written summary
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Review and edit written summary
- [ ] **Issei:** Polish slide design and flow
- [ ] **Taichi:** Verify all numbers and calculations
- [ ] **All:** Full deck walkthrough and feedback session (1.5 hours)

### Phase 4: Practice & Refinement (Days 9-10)
**Goal:** Perfect delivery and prepare for Q&A

#### Day 9: Rehearsals
- [ ] **All:** Full presentation rehearsal #1 (with timing)
- [ ] Identify weak points, awkward transitions
- [ ] **Issei:** Coach on delivery techniques
- [ ] **All:** Q&A simulation — brainstorm 20 likely questions
- [ ] Assign question categories to team members
- [ ] **All:** Full presentation rehearsal #2 (refined)

#### Day 10: Final Prep
- [ ] **All:** Final rehearsal #3 (morning)
- [ ] **All:** Q&A rapid-fire practice
- [ ] **Shreshta:** Final proofread of all materials
- [ ] **All:** Tech check (virtual platform, screen sharing, backup plans)
- [ ] **All:** Rest and mental preparation

## Likely Q&A Topics

Prepare responses for these categories:

### Portfolio Construction
- Why this specific asset allocation?
- How did you determine risk tolerance?
- Why these specific funds/vehicles?
- How does this compare to a benchmark?

### Risk Management
- How does the portfolio perform in a market crash?
- What's the maximum drawdown expectation?
- How do you manage inflation risk?
- What's the rebalancing strategy?

### Market Views
- What's your view on interest rates?
- How do you position for recession risk?
- Why overweight/underweight certain sectors?

### Implementation
- How would you implement this portfolio?
- What are the tax implications?
- How do you handle liquidity needs?
- What's the monitoring and reporting cadence?
